who are we?

We dance for Love! We dance for freedom and for justice! We create a space together to celebrate our connections and differences as a venue to learn and unlearn. A poetic space for radical care and radical expression. We are artists and music lovers in Berlin who have come together to form a collective called "Unterwegs." Our goal is to facilitate communication between DJs and clubs, making it easier for them to connect and collaborate. Additionally, we aim to establish ourselves as a comprehensive music genre library in Berlin.
